AI Powered Agriculture

AI Disease Detection

This uses Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning to identify crop diseases. The farmer uploads an image of a crop leaf, and the trained AI model analyzes the image to detect the disease and provides the predicted result. Early disease detection helps farmers reduce crop loss and take preventive action quickly.

Weather intelligence

Weather plays a crucial role in farming. This module provides real-time weather information such as temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind speed. Based on these conditions, the system also provides farming recommendations to help farmers prepare for changing weather.

Chatbot AI

The AI Chatbot serves as a virtual agricultural assistant. Farmers can ask questions in natural language, such as suitable crops, disease management, irrigation methods, or farming practices. The chatbot instantly provides helpful agricultural guidance.
